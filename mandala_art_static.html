Mandala Art Work

My Favourite Pastime

In the ancient Sanskrit language, mandala means “circle.” It is a geometric design that represents the cosmos or deities in various heavenly worlds. “It's all about finding peace in the symmetry of the design and of the universe."

How to draw your own mandala:

  • The first step for any mandala is to make a grid as we want it to be symmetrical and we want all of the patterns to be equal.
  • Then decide what basic shapes you want to radiate from that circle, like an inverted U or V.
  • The next layer should be slightly different. You might change the shape a little, fill it in with different strokes or colors.
